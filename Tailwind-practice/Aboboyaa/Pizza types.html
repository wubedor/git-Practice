Types of Pizza


Margherita Pizza


ingredients:

Pizza dough: Made with flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil. Tomato sauce: Typically made with crushed fresh tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mozzarella cheese: Fresh mozzarella is preferred, often torn or sliced. Fresh basil: Chopped basil leaves added as a topping.


Chicago Pizza


Toppings:

Sausage and Mushroom: Sweet Italian sausage combined with earthy mushrooms, layered over mozzarella and tomato sauce. Spinach and Artichoke: A rich blend of creamy spinach and marinated artichoke hearts atop layers of cheese and sauce.


BBQ Chicken Pizza


Ingredients:

Cheese: Pecorino Romano, ricotta, fresh mozzarella, or Bufala mozzarella Tomato: San Marzano tomatoes Herbs: Basil Oil: Extra virgin olive oil Vegetables: Eggplant, squash blossoms, Romanesco, arugula, mushrooms, olives, artichokes Meats: Prosciutto, sausage, cured meats Eggs: Boiled egg


Veggie Pizza


Ingredients

Flour, Water: A key ingredient in the dough Salt: A key ingredient in the dough Tomatoes: Used to make the sauce Onions: Used to make the sauce Herbs: Used to make the sauce Anchovies: Used to make the sauce Breadcrumbs: Used to top the pizza Cheese: Can be sliced white cheddar or other cheeses.
